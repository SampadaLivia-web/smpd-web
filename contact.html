Sampada Livia Neighbourhood

Sector CHI V is a fast growing locality located on the Yamuna Expressway, which is a 6 lane expressway which connects Greater Noida with Agra. Located between two well - established development hubs of Noida and Greater Noida, the speed corridor is slated to be a hub for residential and commercial properties. With excellent connectivity , Sector CHI V is expected to be the next big attraction in the Real Estate sector along with a host of retail establishments. Apart from Delhi and Agra, Yamuna Expressway connects this place to Noida, Greater Noida, Faridabad, Palwal, Mathura, Hathras and Aligarh. Social infrastructure such as schools, colleges, hospitals and malls, is coming up at a rapid pace. SEZs, Jaypee Sports City and Gautam Buddha University also lie in the vicinity making this area a major hotspot for residential and commercial development. The demand in the IT sector is gaining swiftly in the last few years. now due to metro project, this region is sure to have better connectivity and boost the real estate development in this area. The locality has been witnessing a rise in the number of reputed multinational companies like Metlife, Accenture and TCS due to the convenience it offers. And this has encouraged a large number of developers who have cashed in on the realty boom. Facilities like malls, shopping and commercial complexes, and restaurants have only increased the value of the area.
